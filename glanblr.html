GLANBLR Model Overview

The GLANBLR model (Generative Latent Adaptive Neural Binary Logistic Regressor) helps generate synthetic environmental data, enabling analysts to study rare pollution patterns in lesser-known ports.

Applications:

  • Emission risk analysis
  • Synthetic data generation for gaps
  • Impact visualization for environmental compliance
